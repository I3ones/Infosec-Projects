Election Day Guide: What You Need to Know

Dear citizen,

Election Day is approaching! Here’s what you need to know:

1. Verify Your Registration:

Ensure your registration is up-to-date at Vote.org. If you’ve moved or changed your name, update your information as soon as possible.

2. Now Your Polling Place:

Use the Polling Place Locator on your state’s election website to find your polling location. Locations may have changed since the last election.

3. Identificate Requirements:

Some states require identification to vote. Check the specific ID requirements for your state and bring the necessary documents to the polling place.

4. Review the Ballot:

Familiarize yourself with the candidates and issues on your ballot. Reviewing a sample ballot beforehand can help you vote more efficiently.

5. Plan Your Trip:

Consider transportation options and peak voting hours to avoid long lines.

For more detailed information, visit Vote.org.

Thank you for making your voice heard!
